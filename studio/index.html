The Studio

Tools worth keeping.

Software should feel like a favorite tool — it does its job so cleanly you forget it’s there and just get on with your work.

Fixin Studio is an independent software studio. We build products, not trends: small, sharp tools that solve a real problem for real people. If we wouldn't use it ourselves, we don't build it.

What we believe

  • Solve real problems. Every product starts with an actual problem, not a buzzword.
  • Sweat the details. Typography, loading states, microcopy, accessibility — they’re part of the product.
  • Privacy first. Collect less, store less, respect more. Your data is yours.
  • Think long-term. We optimize for ten years, not ten weeks.

What we don’t build

No crypto. No gambling. No dark patterns. No ad-driven clickbait. No selling customer data. No AI for its own sake. If a feature doesn’t improve your life, it doesn’t ship.

The promise

We will never knowingly ship software that wastes your time. And if we get something wrong, we’ll own it, fix it, and learn from it. Every product should earn its place on your Home Screen.
