BatchSmith › Privacy Policy

Privacy.

BatchSmith stores your data on your device. We have no server, no analytics, and no way to see your recipes, batches, or notes — even if we wanted to.

Last updated: July 2, 2026. Applies to BatchSmith for iOS, published by Fixin Studio LLC.

What we collect

Nothing. BatchSmith does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on servers we control. There is no Fixin Studio backend. There are no analytics. There is no crash reporting. Your data stays with you.

Your recipes and batches

Everything you enter in BatchSmith — recipes, batches, cure logs, notes, and weight records — is stored locally on your device and, if you have iCloud enabled, synced through your personal iCloud account via CloudKit private database.

CloudKit private database means your data is encrypted by Apple and stored in your own iCloud account. Fixin Studio has no access to it, cannot read it, and receives no copy of it. If you delete the app, your data can be deleted from iCloud through your device’s iCloud settings.

In-app purchases

BatchSmith uses Apple’s StoreKit for in-app purchases, processed entirely on your device. We never see your payment information, Apple ID, name, or email address. Purchase verification happens cryptographically on-device — no transaction data is sent to any server we operate.

Apple’s handling of your payment is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

iCloud

If you use iCloud sync, your data is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy. You can disable iCloud sync for BatchSmith at any time in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud.

App Store nutrition label

BatchSmith’s App Store privacy label reflects this policy: Data Not Collected. We make no claim to your data because we genuinely have none of it.

Children

BatchSmith is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we’ll update the date above and note what changed. Questions? hello@fixinstudio.com.
