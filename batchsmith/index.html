BatchSmith.

Cure with confidence.

A precise, practical companion for curing bacon and managing your charcuterie recipes — so every batch comes out right.

BatchSmith takes the guesswork out of curing. Get the salt and cure ratios dialed in, keep your batches and recipes organized, and come back to exactly what worked. Built for people who take their craft seriously, without getting in the way.

What it does

  • Calculate precise cure amounts by weight — no guesswork, no math errors
  • Manage recipes and track every batch from mix to finish
  • Record notes, adjustments, and outcomes for each cure
  • Calculate target weight loss to know when your cure is done
  • Keep your whole charcuterie library organized in one place
